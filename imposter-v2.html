How to Play

Objective

Crewmates know the secret word. The Imposter only knows the category hint. Find the Imposter before they blend in!

Rules

01Minimum 3 players required to start.
02The host sets a secret word and a category hint.
03Roles are dealt secretly — one random player is the Imposter.
04Everyone takes turns giving one-word or one-sentence clues about the word.
05Vote to eliminate someone. If you catch the Imposter — Crew wins! If they survive — Imposter wins.
